## Who this is for

Manufacturing and operations teams who need to understand and apply AI, without becoming data scientists first — plant managers, reliability engineers, maintenance leads, and operations executives.

## Programs

### Foundations of Industrial AI
A half-day or full-day introduction to what AI can and can't do on the plant floor, with real case studies.

### Applied Reliability & AI Workshop
A deeper, hands-on program using the RCAI Framework to build an AI adoption roadmap specific to your operation.

### Executive Briefing
A condensed session for leadership teams evaluating AI investment across manufacturing operations.
